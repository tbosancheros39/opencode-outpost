## COMPANY OVERVIEW

Clickem is a Meta and Google Ads management agency based in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. A three-person team — Mirza Lačević (CEO/Strategist), Elvir (Operations), and Adnan (Account Management) — manages $200k+ in monthly ad spend across 300+ active client accounts.

We work with eCommerce brands, DTC companies, and growth-stage businesses in the US and European markets. Our clients typically generate $10k–$500k in monthly revenue and are ready to invest seriously in paid media.

Our model is straightforward: we take on clients we believe in, structure pricing around their growth targets, and hold ourselves accountable to what moves — ROAS, CPA, and revenue. We don't run campaigns for the sake of running campaigns.

Results we've delivered for clients:
— Infraheal: 500% ROAS, 68% CPA reduction
— Eternal Roses: 500% ROAS, 90% CPA reduction in 90 days
— MD Auto: 50% sales increase in 4 months

Our work is concentrated in Meta Ads (60%) with a strong secondary focus on Google Ads (30%) and Conversion Rate Optimization (10%). Every account gets direct senior attention — no junior staff or bloated account structures.

## SERVICES OFFERED

**Meta Ads Management — 60%**
Full-funnel Meta Ads strategy and execution: audience research, creative direction, campaign structuring, daily optimization, and performance reporting. We manage campaigns across Facebook and Instagram for eCommerce, lead generation, and app install objectives.

**Google Ads Management — 30%**
Search, Display, and Performance Max campaigns. We build accounts from the ground up or clean up and scale existing Google Ads setups. Focus areas: ROAS-based bidding, keyword strategy, and landing page alignment.

**Conversion Rate Optimization — 10%**
We audit and optimize the funnel between ad click and conversion. Landing page analysis, form flow improvements, and A/B test design — working alongside clients' existing development resources.
